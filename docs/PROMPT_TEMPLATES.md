## Customization Notes

### **When to Customize**

These templates are designed to work as-is, but you may adjust imagery if:
- Your track has a specific sub-theme (e.g., "ocean night" vs "city night")
- You want to emphasize a particular mood element
- Your visual aesthetic requires specific references

### **How to Customize Safely**

**✅ You CAN change:**
- Imagery component only (2nd position in 4-part structure)
- Example: "deep night calm" → "quiet ocean night" (stay within approved vocabulary)

**❌ DO NOT change:**
- Texture/timbre (1st position) - keep from approved list
- Harmonic element (3rd position) - keep from approved list
- Motion quality (4th position) - keep from approved list
- Anchor phrase - ALWAYS keep `— vaporwave synthwave lofi`

### **Custom Imagery Rules**

When creating custom imagery:
1. Use evocative, not technical language
2. Stay positive (no "without", "no", "zero")
3. Match the emotional tone of your track
4. Keep it concise (2-4 words max)
5. Test similarity: if custom imagery drops match scores below 70%, revert to template
